Mobile App Work

Smart phones are everywhere now a days. With Android accounting for 70% of the market in the 4th quarter of 2013, I decided to brush up on my mobile app skills. In doing so, an opportunity came up within my energy-intelligence startup allowing me to develop and release my 1st polished product to the market.

I developed a custom sliding menu, animated bar graph, and all the views needed for the app. Multiple threads were used to keep processed moving smoothly. Push notifications were also integrated using the GCM library. Android fragmentation was delt with by setting up different dimension files that would be referenced depending on the screen size.
